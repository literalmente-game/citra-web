Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon

Rating
Bad
Game functions, but with major graphical or audio glitches. Unable to progress in specific areas due to glitches even with workarounds.
Type
Game Icon
Title IDs
0004000000055F00
System Files Required? N/A
Shared Font Required? N/A

The game runs smoothly in menus and without major performance drops ingame. It can be rendered at higher resolutions without losing much in terms of performance. It stutters a bit almost every time you go through a door or start sucking up a ghost. The game can now be played from start to finish without crashing. Note that a fairly high-end PC may be required to obtain decent performance; low-end GPUs or CPUs with integrated graphics cards will almost certainly give terrible performance.
