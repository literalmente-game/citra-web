Summary

Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon work great in Citra. Graphically, the games look amazing with minor graphical issues, but require a decent hardware to achieve higher frame rates. Flaws with Intel GPU’s require some users with this hardware to disable hardware shaders, to avoid crashes at cutscenes.
